Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting the 'My Orders' section on your account page. You'll also receive real-time updates via email and SMS once your order ships.

2. How do I return a fashion item?

We offer easy returns. Simply go to 'My Orders', select the item you wish to return, and follow the return steps. Check our Return Policy for more details.

3. Can I exchange an item for a different size or color?

If the product is eligible for exchange, you can exchange it for a different size or color through the 'My Orders' section. Refer to our Exchange Policy for more information.

4. How long does delivery take for fashion items?

Delivery time depends on your location and the product’s availability. Standard delivery takes between 3-7 business days. Express delivery options are available at checkout.

5. Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards for fashion lovers. You can buy a gift card on our website and redeem it for purchases on any fashion items.

6. How can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it. If your order has already been shipped, please contact customer support for assistance.

7. How do I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7. You can reach out through the 'Contact Us' section or directly at support@fashionstore.com.
