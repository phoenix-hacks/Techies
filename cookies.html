1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and actions over time, making your browsing experience more efficient and personalized.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  1. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the functioning of our website. They enable basic features such as page navigation and access to secure areas of the site.
  2. Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors interact with our website, helping us improve performance and user experience.
  3. Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences, such as language settings, to enhance your experience on our platform.
  4. Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to track your browsing habits and show you relevant advertisements based on your interests.

3. How We Use Cookies

FashionQuick uses cookies to:

  1. Enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and settings.
  2. Provide personalized content, advertisements, and promotions.
  3. Analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve our platform.
  4. Ensure the proper functioning of our services and features.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. However, please note that if you disable certain cookies, you may not be able to use some parts of our platform, and your user experience may be affected. Here are the steps to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  1. Google Chrome: Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data.
  2. Mozilla Firefox: Go to Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  3. Safari: Go to Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and Website Data.
  4. Microsoft Edge: Go to Settings > Privacy, Search, and Services > Cookies and Site Permissions.
